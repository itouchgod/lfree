MMH is built on a simple rule: **your files stay on your device**.

## No silent uploads

We don't sync media to our servers. Encryption and vault logic run locally.

## Transparent permissions

macOS permissions are requested only when needed, with clear in-app explanations.

## Future sync (optional)

If iCloud or peer sync ever ships, it will be explicit, opt-in, and documented.
