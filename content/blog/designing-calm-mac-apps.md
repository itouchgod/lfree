Great utility software disappears into your workflow. Here's what we optimize for:

## Visual quiet

- Dark graphite surfaces with cool blue accents
- Generous padding — never cram controls
- Subtle depth instead of flashy motion

## Interaction

- One primary action per screen
- Predictable keyboard shortcuts
- Fast paths for repeat tasks

Calm design is not minimalism for its own sake — it's respect for attention.
