FileNest is a lightweight macOS tool for collecting, tagging, and opening your most-used folders — from the main window or the menu bar. Everything stays on your Mac.

> **Current version: v0.1.3** · Now available

## Download

Choose the build that matches your Mac:

| Mac type | Package |
|----------|---------|
| **Apple Silicon** (M1 / M2 / M3 / M4) | [FileNest-mac-arm64.zip](https://github.com/itouchgod/filenest/releases/download/v0.1.3/FileNest-mac-arm64.zip) |
| **Intel** | [FileNest-mac-x64.zip](https://github.com/itouchgod/filenest/releases/download/v0.1.3/FileNest-mac-x64.zip) |

DMG installers are also on the [GitHub release page](https://github.com/itouchgod/filenest/releases/tag/v0.1.3).

Not sure which chip you have? Open **About This Mac** and check **Chip** or **Processor**.

You can also download from the [FileNest product page](/apps/filenest).

## Install

1. Download and unzip the `.zip` file (or open the `.dmg` and drag **FileNest.app** to Applications)
2. Open FileNest from Launchpad
3. If macOS blocks the app, go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **Open Anyway**

## Features

- **Folder library** — Drag folders in and keep them in one clean list
- **Tags** — Group entries (e.g. Work, Website, AI Creation)
- **Favorites** — Pin the folders you open most often
- **Menu bar** — Quick access without switching apps
- **Recent history** — Jump back to folders you opened lately
- **Backup** — Export and import your library as JSON

## System requirements

- macOS 13 Ventura or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
